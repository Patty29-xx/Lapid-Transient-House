Wright Park

Wright Park in Baguio is a popular park known for its iconic pool of pines and horseback riding activities, offering a scenic escape in the heart of the city. It's a must-visit for tourists looking for outdoor fun, photo opportunities, and a chance to experience the cool, refreshing mountain air.

The Mansion, Romulo Dr, Baguio, Benguet

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio is a beautiful, well-maintained park featuring a wide array of plants and flowers, as well as traditional Igorot huts. It's a must-visit for tourists looking to enjoy the city's natural beauty, immerse in local culture, and relax in a serene environment.

37 Leonard Wood Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Bamboo Sanctuary

The Bamboo Sanctuary in Baguio is a peaceful haven showcasing a variety of bamboo species amidst lush greenery, perfect for nature lovers. It's a must-visit for tourists seeking a tranquil escape, offering a chance to connect with nature and enjoy the calming ambiance of bamboo groves.

Pacdal Liteng, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

The Mansion

The Mansion in Baguio is a historic landmark and the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines, known for its grand architecture and beautiful gardens. It’s a must-visit for tourists interested in history, culture, and stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

The Mansion, Romulo Dr, Baguio, Benguet

Minesview Park

Mines View Park in Baguio offers stunning panoramic views of the mountains and the historic mining town of Itogon, making it a perfect spot for photography and relaxation. It's a must-visit for tourists looking to experience Baguio's natural beauty, local crafts, and cool climate all in one place.

Mines View Park, Baguio City, 2600

Camp John hay

Camp John Hay in Baguio is a former American military base turned into a popular leisure and nature destination, offering scenic views, hiking trails, and various recreational activities. It's a must-visit for tourists looking to enjoy a blend of history, nature, and outdoor adventure in a cool, peaceful environment.

Camp John Hay, Baguio, Benguet

Burnham Park

Burnham Park in Baguio is a popular urban park known for its picturesque man-made lake, boating activities, and beautiful walking paths. It’s a must-visit for tourists seeking a relaxing escape, family-friendly activities, and a chance to enjoy the cool breeze of Baguio’s serene environment.

Jose Abad Santos Dr, Baguio, 2600 Benguet
