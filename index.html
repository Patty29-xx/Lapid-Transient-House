Explore Attractions Nearby

Tourist Spots

Wright Park

Wright Park in Baguio is a popular park known for its iconic pool of pines and horseback riding activities, offering a scenic escape in the heart of the city. It's a must-visit for tourists looking for outdoor fun, photo opportunities, and a chance to experience the cool, refreshing mountain air.

The Mansion, Romulo Dr, Baguio, Benguet

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio is a beautiful, well-maintained park featuring a wide array of plants and flowers, as well as traditional Igorot huts. It's a must-visit for tourists looking to enjoy the city's natural beauty, immerse in local culture, and relax in a serene environment.

37 Leonard Wood Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Bamboo Sanctuary

The Bamboo Sanctuary in Baguio is a peaceful haven showcasing a variety of bamboo species amidst lush greenery, perfect for nature lovers. It's a must-visit for tourists seeking a tranquil escape, offering a chance to connect with nature and enjoy the calming ambiance of bamboo groves.

Pacdal Liteng, Baguio, 2600 Benguet
